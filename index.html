'Overall Process

Step One: Cleaning house

The first step is deciding if you will be keeping anything from the old feed or throwing everything out.

Step Two: Find Inspiration

The next step is checking out profiles that fit the aesthetic and overall feel that you wish to emulate on your account. when you find them write what sticks out to you and how you could create something similar in realm but not the same.

Step Three: Create and implement

For this step you will need to assemble the content you ultimately wish to put out and practice putting it on test grids to see how the layout will look.
